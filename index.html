FAQ

Questions before you record?

Where is VIRGO Podcast Studio located?

VIRGO Podcast Studio is located at 1345 Avenue of the Americas in Midtown Manhattan, New York City.

Can I record a video podcast?

Yes. The studio is built for video podcast recording with cameras, lighting, microphones, and a multi-camera workflow.

Do I need podcast experience?

No. The setup is designed to be simple for first-time podcasters, business teams, and experienced creators.

Can you help with editing or clips?

Yes. Editing and short-form clips can be discussed depending on the recording and final deliverables you need.

Can comedians record stand-up routines?

Yes. Comedians can record stand-up routines, joke rehearsals, crowd work, and short-form comedy clips for social media, submissions, and promo reels.
